每日学习流程

按照“破译→扩词→场景→写作→口语→小测→复盘卡”的闭环完成每天训练。

①

破译：词根、词缀、拼写规律、易混点

从结构和规律入手，先看懂单词为什么这样拼、这样变。

②

扩词：核心词扩展成词族

用一个核心词带出名词、动词、形容词和常用短语。

③

场景：放入教育、科技、环境、健康、社会等 IELTS 场景

把新词连接到 IELTS 高频话题，避免只会背不会用。

④

写作：生成可直接用于作文的句子

训练正式、清楚、可迁移的作文表达。

⑤

口语：生成可直接用于口语的句子

训练自然、具体、能直接回答问题的口语句子。

⑥

小测：英译中、中译英、选词填空、造句

用多种题型检查理解、拼写和输出能力。

⑦

复盘卡：错词复活卡、词族卡、场景表达卡

把当天最容易遗忘的内容放进复习系统。

② 破译

able / ability

  • able 是形容词，常用结构是 be able to do sth.，表示“能够做某事”。
  • ability 是名词，表示“能力”，常搭配 improve/develop/show ability。

act / action / active / activity / actor / actress

  • act 表示“行动；表演”，是这一组的核心。
  • action 是“行动/措施”；active 是“积极的/活跃的”；activity 是“活动”。
  • actor 是男演员或泛指演员；actress 是女演员。

advice / advise

  • advice 是名词“建议”，通常不可数：a piece of advice。
  • advise 是动词“建议”，常用 advise sb. to do sth.。

agree / agreement / against

  • agree 是动词“同意”；agreement 是名词“同意/协议”。
  • against 表示“反对”，常与 agree 形成观点对比：agree with / be against。

alone / lonely

  • alone 强调客观状态“独自一人”，不一定难过。
  • lonely 强调主观感受“孤独的”，带情绪色彩。

③ 扩词

核心词 词族扩展 常用搭配
able able, ability, be able to improve ability, be able to communicate, learning ability
act act, action, active, activity, actor, actress take action, stay active, outdoor activity
advise advise, advice, adviser give advice, follow advice, advise students to read aloud
agree agree, agreement, disagree, against agree with a view, reach an agreement, be against the rule

④ 场景

教育

Students can improve their learning ability when teachers give clear advice and allow them to answer aloud.

个人成长

People are more likely to achieve their goals if they take action and stay active every day.

社会规则

Although some people are against strict rules, an agreement can appear when both sides communicate calmly.

⑤ 写作

  1. Education should help students develop the ability to think independently and take action in real situations.
  2. Although online learning is convenient, students still need active communication with teachers and classmates.
  3. Governments should allow young people to join community activities because these experiences can build confidence.
  4. Many people are afraid of failure, but useful advice can help them achieve long-term goals.
  5. A clear agreement between schools and parents can provide better answers to students' learning problems.

⑥ 口语

  1. I think my English ability has improved because I read aloud and answer questions every day.
  2. I am not afraid of making mistakes, as mistakes can show me what I need to practise.
  3. My teacher advised me to stay active in class, and I already feel more confident.
  4. I usually study alone in the evening, but I do not feel lonely because I have a clear goal.
  5. It was amazing to see how small daily actions helped me achieve better results.

选词填空 5题

备选词：active / advice / alone / agree / achieve

  1. Students should stay _____ during group discussions.
  2. Good _____ can help learners avoid common mistakes.
  3. I prefer to study _____ when I need deep focus.
  4. Many people _____ that exercise is important for health.
  5. It takes time and effort to _____ a big goal.

⑧ 复盘卡

错词复活卡

  • advice：不可数名词，不说 an advice；可说 a piece of advice。
  • although：后面接完整句，不和 but 同时使用。
  • alone：强调独自一人；不要直接等同 lonely。

词族卡

  • able → ability → be able to
  • act → action → active → activity → actor → actress
  • agree → agreement；against 表示反对方向

IELTS场景表达卡

  • 教育：improve learning ability / give useful advice / answer aloud
  • 成长：take action / achieve a goal / be afraid of failure
  • 社会：reach an agreement / be against a policy / allow public discussion

复盘卡

把错词、词族和场景表达集中记录，方便隔天、三天后、一周后反复复活。
