每日学习流程

按照“词汇清单→破译→扩词→中考场景→写作→口语→小测+复盘卡”的闭环完成每天训练。

①

词汇清单：英文、中文、词性、中考常用搭配

先明确每个词的基础意思、词性和中考常见用法。

②

破译：词根、词缀、拼写规律、易混点

从结构和规律入手，先看懂单词为什么这样拼、这样变。

③

扩词：核心词扩展成词族

用一个核心词带出名词、动词、形容词和常用短语。

④

中考场景：学习成长、校园生活、家庭朋友、健康运动、环保、旅行、科技、传统文化、梦想计划

把新词连接到中考英语高频话题，避免只会背不会用。

⑤

写作：生成中考作文可套句

训练正式、清楚、可迁移的中考作文表达。

⑥

口语：生成能直接说出口的句子

训练自然、具体、能直接用于日常交流的口语句子。

⑦

小测+复盘卡：英译中、中译英、选词填空、造句、错词复活卡

用多种题型检查理解、拼写和输出能力，并把错词放进复习系统。

Although English is difficult, I believe I can improve my ability and achieve my dream.

② 破译

able / ability

  • able 是形容词，常用结构是 be able to do sth.，表示“能够做某事”。
  • ability 是名词，表示“能力”，常搭配 improve/develop/show ability。

act / action / active / activity / actor / actress

  • act 表示“行动；表演”，是这一组的核心。
  • action 是“行动/措施”；active 是“积极的/活跃的”；activity 是“活动”。
  • actor 是男演员或泛指演员；actress 是女演员。

advice / advise

  • advice 是名词“建议”，通常不可数：a piece of advice。
  • advise 是动词“建议”，常用 advise sb. to do sth.。

agree / agreement / against

  • agree 是动词“同意”；agreement 是名词“同意/协议”。
  • against 表示“反对”，常与 agree 形成观点对比：agree with / be against。

alone / lonely

  • alone 强调客观状态“独自一人”，不一定难过。
  • lonely 强调主观感受“孤独的”，带情绪色彩。

③ 扩词

核心词 词族扩展 常用搭配
able able, ability, be able to improve ability, be able to communicate, learning ability
act act, action, active, activity, actor, actress take action, stay active, outdoor activity
advise advise, advice, adviser give advice, follow advice, advise students to read aloud
agree agree, agreement, disagree, against agree with a view, reach an agreement, be against the rule

④ 场景

教育

Students can improve their learning ability when teachers give clear advice and allow them to answer aloud.

个人成长

People are more likely to achieve their dreams if they take action and stay active every day.

社会规则

Although some people are against strict rules, an agreement can appear when both sides communicate calmly.

⑤ 写作

  1. Students should improve their learning ability by reading aloud every day.
  2. Although English is difficult, I believe I can improve my ability and achieve my dream.
  3. Parents should give children useful advice instead of getting angry.
  4. Being active in class can help students understand knowledge better.
  5. Some people agree with this idea, but others are against it.

⑥ 口语

  1. I want to improve my English ability.
  2. I usually read English aloud in the morning.
  3. My teacher gave me some useful advice.
  4. I think I should be more active in class.
  5. I hope I can achieve my dream through hard work.

⑧ 复盘卡

错词复活卡

  • advice：不可数名词，不说 an advice；可说 a piece of advice。
  • although：后面接完整句，不和 but 同时使用。
  • alone：强调独自一人；不要直接等同 lonely。
  • Although English is difficult, I will never give up.

词族卡

  • able → ability → be able to
  • act → action → active → activity → actor → actress
  • agree → agreement；against 表示反对方向
  • advise → advice
  • achieve → achievement

中考场景表达卡

  • 教育：improve learning ability / give useful advice / answer aloud
  • 成长：take action / achieve a dream / be afraid of failure
  • 社会：reach an agreement / be against a policy / allow public discussion

Day 2 核心句跟读

点击小喇叭按钮，跟读英文核心句 3 遍。

Believe in yourself, begin with basic steps, and build a bright future.

① 词汇清单

  • background
    词性：名词；中文：背景；搭配：family background
  • basic
    词性：形容词；中文：基本的；搭配：basic skills
  • beautiful
    词性：形容词；中文：美丽的；搭配：beautiful scenery
  • become
    词性：动词；中文：变成；搭配：become better
  • begin
    词性：动词；中文：开始；搭配：begin to learn
  • behavior
    词性：名词；中文：行为；搭配：good behavior
  • believe
    词性：动词；中文：相信；搭配：believe in
  • below
    词性：介词或副词；中文：在下面；搭配：below zero
  • beside
    词性：介词；中文：在旁边；搭配：sit beside me
  • besides
    词性：介词或副词；中文：除……之外；搭配：besides English
  • better
    词性：形容词或副词；中文：更好的；搭配：do better
  • between
    词性：介词；中文：在两者之间；搭配：between classes
  • beyond
    词性：介词；中文：超出；搭配：beyond my ability
  • bicycle
    词性：名词；中文：自行车；搭配：ride a bicycle
  • birth
    词性：名词；中文：出生；搭配：date of birth
  • borrow
    词性：动词；中文：借入；搭配：borrow books
  • brave
    词性：形容词；中文：勇敢的；搭配：be brave
  • bright
    词性：形容词；中文：明亮的；搭配：bright future
  • bring
    词性：动词；中文：带来；搭配：bring happiness
  • build
    词性：动词；中文：建造；搭配：build confidence

② 破译

拼写规律

  • be 开头常表示状态变化：become、begin、believe。
  • bi 有“两”的含义，bicycle 可理解为“两轮车”。
  • bright 既可写光线明亮，也可写前途光明：bright future。

易混点

  • beside 表示“在旁边”；besides 表示“此外”。
  • borrow 是“借入”；lend 是“借出”。
  • between 多用于两者之间；among 多用于三者或以上之间。

③ 扩词

词族扩展

  • beauty → beautiful → beautifully
  • begin → beginning
  • behave → behavior
  • believe → belief → believable
  • build → building → builder

高频短语

  • believe in yourself
  • begin with basic steps
  • borrow a book from the library
  • bring people together
  • build a bright future

④ 场景

校园生活

Students can borrow books from the library and build good reading habits.

个人成长

If we believe in ourselves and begin with basic steps, we can become better.

旅行见闻

Beautiful scenery and brave people can bring us unforgettable memories.

⑤ 写作

作文可套句

  • At the beginning, I was afraid, but I decided to be brave.
  • Good behavior can bring warmth to our class and make everyone feel respected.
  • Reading not only broadens my background knowledge but also helps me build confidence.

小段落输出

I believe small changes can bring a better life. I will begin with basic habits, borrow useful books, and build a bright future step by step.

⑥ 口语

日常表达

  • May I borrow your bicycle?
  • Please sit beside me.
  • Besides English, I also enjoy science.
  • The temperature is below zero today.

问答训练

  • What do you want to become in the future?
  • I want to become a person who can bring help to others.

⑧ 复盘卡

错词复活卡

  • beside：地点在旁边；besides：除此之外。
  • borrow：从别人那里借入；不要和 lend 混淆。
  • Besides English, I borrow science books to build my background knowledge.

词族卡

  • beauty → beautiful → beautifully
  • begin → beginning
  • build → building → builder
  • behave → behavior

中考场景表达卡

  • 校园：borrow books / build habits / sit beside me
  • 成长：believe in yourself / become better / be brave
  • 未来：bright future / build confidence / bring hope
